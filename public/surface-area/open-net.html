What is surface area?
It is the total area covering the outside of a 3D shape.

How to explain it

  1. A cube has 6 faces.
  2. Each face is a square.
  3. If the side length is s, then one face has area s × s.
  4. Since all 6 faces are the same, multiply by 6.
  5. So the formula is 6s².
