Proportions & Ratios · Grade 6

Percent Means Out of 100

Learn the idea first, then solve a snack-vote example using a 100-square picture.

By the end of this lesson you will be able to tell what a percent means and find a percent of a number.

%
The Concept Definition
35% = 35 out of 100
Percent Percent means "for every 100." So 35% means 35 out of every 100 equal parts.
Whole The whole is the full group, full price, or full amount.
Part The part is the piece you are finding.
1
The Rule Plain steps
1Change the percent to a fraction out of 100.
2Multiply that fraction by the whole.
3The answer is the part.
Shortcut: 25% means 25/100. That is the same as one fourth.
2
I Do: Snack Vote Example Worked example

Question

A class has 28 students. In a snack vote, 25% chose popcorn. How many students chose popcorn?

Steps

25% means 25 out of 100.
25/100 is the same as 1/4.
One fourth of 28 is 28 ÷ 4 = 7.
So 7 students chose popcorn.
